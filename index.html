Did a text ever pop up in your phone that made you laugh out loud, or brought on a soft smile? Did you get mad at some texts while just going awww at others? May be sometimes when texts popped in, it felt like too many things are going on, or at other times, waiting for a reply made you anxious of the worst sort.

Well, with phonepop, we want to try and tell texting stories of our world with the texting emotions of our lives.

The first phonepop book that we are working on is titled `Flirting With The Wrong Men`, and it is kind of taking good shape so far. Like, we are about 50% super excited and very confident around what we are doing, and about 50% shit scared.
